Knight

The Knight:

The knight is a very useful and unpredictable piece. Its movements are unique from those of the other pieces--it moves in an L-shape: two squares on a rank or file, and then one square perpendicularly. Unlike the other chess pieces, the knight can jump over pieces. Because of its L-shaped maneuvers, it is a very useful attacker and defender. As you play through the annotated games below on your own chessboard, pay attention to how the knights are used astutely by the great chessmasters.

Knight specifications:

  • Point value: 3
  • Movement/Attack range: 2 then 1, in an L-shape in any direction
  • Notation symbol: N
